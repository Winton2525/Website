Naruto
Uzumaki

"I never go back on my word. That's my Nindo, my Ninja way!" - Naruto Uzumaki

Goal: To become the Hokage. A ninja legend.

Description: A young Ninja with the sprit of the malevolent fox demon - The Nine Tails - trapped within him.
Naruto is a blonde, spiky haired, young boy with a chip on his sholder after being ostracised by his community.
To be accepted and respected, he resolves himself to become Konoha's Hokage and surpass all previous leaders, no matter the difficulties.
Naruto, while on his path, forms the friendship his initally lacked and despite the hardships he faces, he eventually receives the acknowledgement he deserves. Naruto is proclaimed the Hero of the Hidden Leaf and eventually the Seventh Hokage!

Family: Naruto's father Minato Namikaze, the Fourth Hokage and his mother Kushina Uzumaki, the previous jinchuriki of The Nine Tails - died the same day Naruto was born. Naruto was raised by his community, though under the watchful eyes of the Elders.
Naruto's Godfather Jiraiya, the famed "Toad Sage" and legendary Sanin took charge of Naruto when he reached the age of 12 - teaching him his ninja ways becoming Naruto's Master - like his father before him.

Later Years: Naruto became Hokage at 29 years old, meaning he was one of the youngest Kage in history. He married Hinata Hyuga and together they have 2 children, Boruto and Himawari Uzumaki.
Naruto is considered the most powerful Ninja in the current age. He has mastered all five Chakra Natures and surpassed all previous Kage. He has achieved his goals and is happy.
